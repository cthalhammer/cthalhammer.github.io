## Abstract

We establish a new, real-space formula for the Zak phase for one dimensional
periodic Jacobi operators in terms of the Weyl m+-function that does not rely on Floquet-
Bloch theory. This novel representation highlights the dependence of the Zak phase on
boundary terms. Moreover, we show how to recover the classical quantisation of the Zak
phase for periodic Jacobi operators with inversion symmetric fundamental cells.
